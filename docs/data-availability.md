# Data Availability

[Home](../index.html) | [Method](method.md) | [Scoring](scoring.md) | [Dimensions](dimensions.md) | [Results](results.md) | [Measures](measures-and-references.md)

Raw participant data and narrative responses are not posted publicly.

This includes:

- interview transcripts,
- open-ended narrative responses,
- Prolific exports,
- Qualtrics exports,
- participant IDs,
- private study logs,
- internal notes,
- raw JSON records,
- raw CSV, TSV, SAV, RDS, or XLSX exports.

Do not add raw data, transcripts, participant exports, or participant-level files to this public repository.

Any sharing of additional information is subject to Rice IRB requirements, participant consent, mentor approval, and de-identification.
